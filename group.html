Suguman Bansal

BansalLab works on building trustworthy AI by using Formal Methods and Programming Languages in AI and Machine Learning applications. Currently, our work is focussed in two broad directions:
  • Formal Specifications in Reinforcement Learning: Read more from our 2022 SAS Keynote Talk.
  • Synthesis and Verification of Finite-Horizon Tasks: Read more from our 2023 ATVA Best Paper.
