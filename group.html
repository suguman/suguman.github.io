BansalLab

BansalLab works on building Trustworthy AI by simplifying the design-development-deployment pipeline of safe and reliable AI systems. We apply Formal Methods and Logic to Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Programming Languages. Our current research thrusts include:

  • Formal Methods for Reinforcement Learning [SAS 2022 Keynote, AAAI 2023 Tutorial]
  • Symbolic Quantitative Reasoning [IJCAI23, AAAI22, TACAS21, CAV19, CAV18b, FoSSaCS18]
  • Synthesis and Verification from High-Level Specifications [ATVA 2023 Best Paper, CAV21, POPL20, AAAI20, CAV18a]
