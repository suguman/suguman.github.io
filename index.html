Suguman Bansal

I am a CRA/NSF Computing Innovation Postdoctoral Fellow with Prof. Rajeev Alur at the Department of Computer and Information Sciences at University of Pennsylvania. I completed my Ph.D under Prof. Moshe Y. Vardi at Rice University.

I am on the Academic Faculty Job Market.
Application Materials: [Research Statement] [Teaching Statement] [Diversity Statement] [Resume]

My research vision is to make AI trustworthy by close integration of formal methods and programming languages with artificial intelligence and machine learning. Towards trustworthy computing, my research has focused on simplifying the design-development-deployment pipeline of safe and reliable AI systems. I have worked on automated controller synthesis, i.e., the automated generation of control systems from a given high-level specification with theoretical guarantees of correctness. My current research thrusts include:

  • Formal Methods for Reinforcement Learning [NeurIPS21, SafeRL21, Invited]
  • Automata-Based Quantitative Reasoning [AAAI22, TACAS21, CAV19, CAV18b, FoSSaCS18]
  • Reactive Synthesis from High-Level Specifications [CAV21, POPL20, AAAI20, CAV18a]
