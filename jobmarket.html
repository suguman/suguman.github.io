    • (Mon - Fri) January 10-14, 2022 One screening interview each day of the week. Recovered from last week by establishing a System. The System inlcudes:
      • Spend 4-5 hrs in preparation for each interview. Emphasized on preparing about the university and the interviewers than my own research.
      • Asked for list of interviewers well in advance. Prepared notes and talking points with everyone. These notes were resourceful during the full interviews also.
      • Prepared answers for the standard screening interview questions: Current and Future reserach, teaching interests at undergraduate and graduate level, new courses, collaborations, why university X.
        • Prepared questions for the intervierews: Graduate student policies and requirements, junior faculty mentorship, compute resources, department growth plans. Asking the same questions at all places helped me gauge universities against each other.
      • Write notes about interview experience and observations immediately after each interview. These notes proved useful during final decision stages.
      • Block an hour before and after the interview.
      • Keep water, coffee, and munchies within reach.
          • All screening interviews went very well. Out of this week, I was invited for the full interview by all but one place.
